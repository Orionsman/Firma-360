# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

CepteCari helps users manage receivables, payables, customers, suppliers, products, sales, payments, reminders, and stock records.

## Data We Collect

- Account information such as email address and authentication data
- Company profile information such as company name, tax number, address, phone, and email
- Business records that the user creates in the app, including customers, suppliers, products, sales, payments, stock movements, and collection reminders
- Basic technical information required to keep the service secure and working

## How We Use Data

- To create and manage user accounts
- To store and sync business records created by the user
- To secure access to company data
- To provide support and investigate issues
- To comply with legal obligations

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data is shared only with service providers required to operate the app, such as hosting, authentication, notifications, and database infrastructure.

## Data Retention

We keep account and business data while the account remains active. If the user deletes the account, we delete or anonymize data unless retention is required for legal, tax, fraud-prevention, or security reasons.

## Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect account and business data. No system can guarantee absolute security.

## User Choices

- Users can update company information inside the app
- Users can change their password inside the app
- Users can delete their account inside the app

## Store Usage

This policy can be published as a public HTTPS page from the Expo web build and used in App Store Connect and Google Play Console.
