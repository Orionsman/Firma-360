# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

CepteCari helps users manage receivables, payables, customers, suppliers, products, sales, and payments. This policy is written so it can be published on a public website before App Store and Google Play submission.

## Data We Collect

- Account information such as email address and encrypted authentication data
- Company profile information such as company name, tax number, address, phone, and email
- Business records that you create in the app, including customers, suppliers, products, sales, payments, and stock movements
- Basic technical information needed to keep the service secure and working

## How We Use Data

- To create and manage your account
- To store and sync the business records you create
- To secure access to company data
- To provide support and investigate issues
- To comply with legal obligations

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data is shared only with service providers needed to operate the app, such as hosting, authentication, and database infrastructure.

## Data Retention

We keep account and business data while your account remains active. If you request deletion, we begin the deletion workflow and remove or anonymize data unless we must keep certain records for legal, tax, fraud-prevention, or security reasons.

## Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect account and business data. No system can guarantee absolute security.

## Your Choices

- You can update company profile information inside the app
- You can request account deletion inside the app from the Settings screen
- You can also request deletion through the public account deletion page that should be linked in store listings before release
