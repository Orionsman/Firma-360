# KVKK Notice

Last updated: April 1, 2026

## Data Controller Context

The operator of CepteCari acts as the data controller for account, company, and business records processed within the app.

## Data Categories

- Account and contact data
- Company profile data
- Customer and supplier records
- Product, stock, sales, payment, and reminder records
- Technical data required for security, authentication, and service delivery

## Processing Purposes

- Account creation and authentication
- Company data synchronization
- Business record storage
- Security, fraud prevention, and auditability
- Support and issue resolution
- Notification delivery
- Compliance with legal obligations

## Transfer and Retention

Data may be shared with infrastructure providers necessary to run the service and with competent authorities where legally required. Data is retained while the account remains active and for any additional retention period required by law, security, or fraud-prevention obligations.

## Data Subject Rights

Users may request information, correction, deletion, or other statutory rights through the support process and in-app account management flows.
