# Compliance Checklist

## Product and Legal

- Privacy Policy published
- Account Deletion page published
- Terms of Service prepared
- KVKK notice prepared
- Support page prepared

- In-app account deletion works
- Password change works

- Google Play Data Safety answers reviewed
- App Store privacy answers reviewed

- Demo account prepared for store review if needed

- Incident and breach response owner defined
- Retention policy reviewed
- Overseas transfer analysis reviewed
- Tax and invoicing process confirmed with accountant
